# Signing in & profile setup

## First time
New volunteers receive a magic-link email. Click the link and you'll land on **Complete Profile** to set a password, phone number, and SMS preferences.

## Returning users
Visit `/login`, enter your email and password. Forgot your password? Use the **Reset password** link.

## Profile checklist
- Full name and phone are required to receive schedule notifications
- Date of birth lets us send a birthday greeting
- SMS opt-in is required to receive text reminders — see the [SMS opt-in rules](#) article
