# Troubleshooting & FAQ

### I'm not receiving roster emails
1. Check spam / promotions.
2. Verify your email under **Profile**.
3. Ask an admin to confirm the team's email setting.

### A volunteer says they're double-booked
The system blocks double-booking on the same date. Check **Profile → Availability** — they may not have blocked the date, or the assignment may have been created before they blocked it.

### An external record didn't sync
Open **Admin → External Sources** and look at the **Last sync** card for that source. If status is **error**, the message is shown inline. Press **Sync now** to retry.

### A message says "no sms opt-in"
The recipient hasn't opted in to SMS. Email them instead, or capture their consent in person and update their profile.

### Where did my pipeline card go?
Cards advance automatically when the source's sequence completes. Check the **External Sources** sequence editor to see which steps have fired.
