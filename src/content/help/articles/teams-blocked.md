# Blocking dates & responding

## Block a date or range
Open **Profile → Availability**. Choose a single date or a date range with optional reason. While blocked, team leads cannot assign you to events on those days — the system will refuse the save.

## Respond to an assignment
When scheduled, you'll get a notification and email. From your **Dashboard**, open the schedule item and choose:
- **Accept** — confirms attendance
- **Decline** — must include a reason

The team lead sees your response immediately.
