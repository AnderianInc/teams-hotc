# Building a roster

Team leads can schedule members from the **Team Dashboard → Schedule** tab.

## Creating an event
1. Open **Schedule → Events** (collapsed list under Schedule).
2. Click **New event**, set date, time, and which teams are serving.
3. Assign members; the system will check whether they have blocked that date and refuse if so.

## Roles
Each team has its own role types (e.g. Greeter, Usher) — manage them under **Team Dashboard → Members → Role types**.

## Notifications
Assigned members receive an in-app notification and (if email is on file) an email. They can **Accept** or **Decline with reason** from their dashboard. Status syncs back to the calendar instantly.
