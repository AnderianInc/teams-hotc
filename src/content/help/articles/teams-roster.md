# Master schedule and team assignments

Admins create the master schedule from **Admin Panel → Teams → Master Schedule**. Team leaders then fill those admin-created dates from **Team Dashboard → Schedule**.

## Admin master schedule
1. Open **Teams → Master Schedule**.
2. Add a service with its name, date, time, notes, and required teams.
3. Open the date to review assignments grouped by team or create an Order of Service run sheet for that scheduled service.

## Team assignments
1. Open your **Team Dashboard → Schedule** tab.
2. Select an admin-created service date assigned to your team.
3. Assign members from your team and choose a role/position as needed.
4. The system checks blocked dates before saving the assignment.

## Roles
Each team has its own role types (e.g. Greeter, Usher) — manage them under **Team Dashboard → Role Types**.

## Notifications
Assigned members receive an in-app notification and (if email is on file) an email. They can **Accept** or **Decline with reason** from their dashboard. Status syncs back to the calendar instantly.
