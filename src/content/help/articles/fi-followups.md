# Logging follow-ups

Every prospect in the pipeline has a follow-up record. Open one to log a call, SMS, email, or in-person visit.

## The auto 'contacted' tag
When you log a contact activity (call / sms / email / in_person / visit), the system automatically adds two tags to the attendee:
- `contacted`
- `contacted:YYYY-MM-DD` (the date of contact)

This lets FI leads filter the pipeline by who has and hasn't been reached recently.

## Pure notes
Logging a **note** does NOT add the contacted tag — use note for internal observations.
