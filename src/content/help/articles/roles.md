# Roles explained

There are four roles in the app. Your role is shown on your profile.

| Role | What they can do |
|------|------------------|
| **Member** | View their own schedule, profile, and team. Respond to roster assignments. |
| **Team Lead** | Everything a member can, plus manage their team's members and roster. |
| **Staff** | Cross-team visibility (ministry data access). |
| **Admin** | Full system access — settings, directory, security, external sources. |

Roles are assigned by admins under **Admin → Settings → Roles**.
