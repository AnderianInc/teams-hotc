# Registering a family

From **Check-In**, tap **Register family** if a child isn't in the system. You'll be prompted for:
- Family last name (used as a shortcut for adding multiple kids)
- Parent / guardian names and phone numbers
- Each child: first name, date of birth (used for auto room assignment), allergies, medical notes

Once saved, the children appear in search results immediately.

## Rooms
Rooms are auto-assigned by age or grade. Admins manage room ranges under **Admin → Settings**.
