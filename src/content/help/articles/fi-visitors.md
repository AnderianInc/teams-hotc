# Registering a visitor

First Impressions team members register new visitors from **First Impressions → Visitors & Members → New Visitor**, or via the public `/welcome` QR code.

## Required fields
- First and last name
- At least one of: phone, email

## Optional but recommended
- Mark **SMS opt-in** only if the visitor verbally or in-writing consented
- Add tags so the visitor lands in the right follow-up flow

When you add a first-time visitor, they're automatically added to the **outreach pipeline** at the **Visited** stage.
