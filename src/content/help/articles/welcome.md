# Welcome to HOTC Volunteer Hub

This app is the operations hub for House of Transformation Church. It brings together volunteer scheduling, the church directory, children's check-in, visitor follow-up, and communications.

## What you can do here
- See and respond to your team schedule
- Update your profile, availability, and SMS preferences
- Welcome visitors and track follow-up
- Check children in and out safely
- Send emails and SMS to members
- View the church organogram

If you get stuck, use the search box at the top of this help center or check the **Troubleshooting & FAQ** page.
